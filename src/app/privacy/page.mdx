# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2024-11-11_

## 1. Introduction

**Mikui** ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use Google OAuth to log in or access our services. By using our services and consenting to Google OAuth, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

When you sign in using Google OAuth, we may collect certain information that is shared with us by Google, including:

- **Basic profile information**: Such as your name, email address, profile picture, and other publicly available information.
- **Additional information**: Any additional information you have consented to share via Google OAuth.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected through Google OAuth is used to:

- Verify and authenticate your identity to provide secure access to our services.
- Personalize your experience on our platform.
- Improve our services and develop new features.
- Communicate with you regarding updates, security alerts, and other administrative messages.

## 4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as necessary to:

- Comply with legal obligations or respond to valid legal processes.
- Protect the rights and safety of our users and our service.
- Facilitate the integration of third-party services that enhance our offerings, provided they are compliant with privacy and security standards.

## 5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Please be aware that, despite our efforts, no security measures are perfect or impenetrable.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide you with access to our services and to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Once your information is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

- Access and update your information via Google settings.
- Revoke access to your Google account through your Google security settings, which will remove our ability to access your Google OAuth data.
- Request deletion of your information from our systems by contacting us.
